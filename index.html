Objective 1: Increase Lead Generation
Focus on maximizing the number of qualified leads for DARE's training programs to feed into Pioneer roles.
KPI 1: Lead Generation Metrics
  • Number of leads generated per campaign and platform (META, TikTok, etc.).
  • Conversion rates from leads to enrolled trainees.
  • Cost per lead (CPL) to measure cost efficiency.
Pilot 1: Weekly Performance Reviews
  • Regularly review campaign performance data.
  • Adjust strategies based on the best-performing creatives, texts, and formats.
Objective 2: Optimize Campaign Performance
  • Ensure all campaigns are cost-efficient.
  • Reach the correct audience.
  • Maximize engagement and conversion rates.
KPI 2: Campaign Performance Metrics
  • Engagement rate (likes, shares, comments).
  • Click-through rate (CTR) for ads.
  • Cost per Engagement (CPE).
Pilot 2: Daily Reporting and Adjustments
  • Use daily reports to monitor the status of lead generation and location-specific needs.
  • Adjust campaign focus (e.g., increase spend in locations with low lead flow).
Objective 3: Monitor Location-Specific Hiring Needs
  • Continuously assess which locations need more talent.
  • Deploy resources to meet these needs effectively.
KPI 3: Location-Specific Hiring Metrics
  • Number of hires needed per location and language.
  • Lead generation success rate per location.
  • Campaign effectiveness in targeted languages.
Pilot 3: Resource Allocation Based on Data
  • Dynamically allocate resources (content creation, ad spend).
  • Target campaigns showing the best lead conversion rates.
Objective 4: Maximize the Effectiveness of Recruitment Events (JRs)
Ensure each recruitment event meets its language needs, location targets, and CV collection goals, aligning with Pioneer's demand for specific skills and talents.
KPI 4: Recruitment Event Metrics
  • Event Attendance Rate: Measure the number of attendees compared to the target number of candidates for each event.
  • Language Match Rate: Track how well the collected CVs meet the language needs specified for each event.
  • CV Collection Target Achievement: Monitor the percentage of the target CVs collected at each event.
  • Event Cost Efficiency: Calculate the average cost per CV collected to ensure events are conducted efficiently.
Pilot 4: Recruitment Event Optimization Strategies
  • Pre-Event Targeting and Promotion: Use targeted promotions to ensure attendance aligns with language and skill requirements for each event.
  • On-Site Feedback and Adjustment: Collect feedback during events to make on-the-spot adjustments.
  • Post-Event Analysis and Reporting: Analyze attendance, language match, and CV collection to refine future events.
  • Dynamic Resource Allocation: Adjust resources dynamically based on event performance data.
